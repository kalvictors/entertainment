NEW CHANNLE FOR UGANDAN MOVIES


It is official; there is a new pay-TV channel dedicated to promote Ugandan film and arts. Pearl Magic TV, which airs on Channel 161 on DStv and Channel 304 on GOtv, rolled off its broadcast on Monday at 4pm in the presence of several industry stakeholders.
DStv has recently rolled out a channel to promote locally made entertainment content. Pearl Magic channel will provide programs like music, reality shows, films and telenovelas.The Marketing Manger for MultiChoice Uganda, Albert Nga stated that he was thrilled about the development which will promote Ugandan talent. Nga further declared, ‘’This channel will include everything that is Ugandan. It will showcase what we are made of and are able to produce.’’ On the other hand, the head of the Association of Producers in Uganda, stated, ‘’This is something we have waited for years. The industry applauds MultiChoice for such an opportunity they have extended to us.’’
